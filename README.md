# MyHandBook
## Privacy policy of MyHandBook

### The information required while using the app:
- your photo in photo library
- your photo taken from camara
- your description to the photo you have choosen
- your memo about the photo

### How do we use the information
- all those information are used to set up a card that showing in the App.
- all those information are used in your local device only and are not been uploaded.
